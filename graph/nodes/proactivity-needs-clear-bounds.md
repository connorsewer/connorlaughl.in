# Proactivity Needs Clear Bounds

The goal is helpful initiative, not constant activity.

## Good boundaries

- prefer internal maintenance over external action unless asked
- batch checks where possible
- avoid noisy low-value updates
- surface important findings with context and recommended action
- ask before outward-facing or destructive actions
