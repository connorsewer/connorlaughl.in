# TSI Marketing Case Studies - Index

## Overview
This folder contains 7 case study stubs documenting Connor Laughlin's work as VP Marketing at TSI. Each stub follows a consistent format: Overview, Key Metrics, What Connor Built, What TSI Marketed, and Attribution Clarity.

## Case Studies

### 1. Outcome-First Messaging Architecture
**File:** `outcome-first-messaging-architecture.md`

Complete repositioning of TSI's go-to-market messaging from feature-centric to outcome-first positioning. Comprehensive 774-line framework with five messaging pillars, business-unit guides, headline banks, and proof library structure.

**Key Proof Points:** $460M 2025E revenue, ~80% digital mix target, 0.003% complaint rate

---

### 2. BDR Pod & Lead Generation Program
**File:** `bdr-pod-lead-generation-program.md`

90-day BDR program design repurposing telesales reps into a lead generation pod. Includes operating model, daily cadence, messaging kits, ABM list strategy, and WebSights signal-to-meeting workflow.

**Key Proof Points:** 40-60 first meetings target, 3-5 weekly meetings per BDR, 100% Tier-A touch SLA

---

### 3. Revenue Funnel KPI Framework
**File:** `revenue-funnel-kpi-framework.md`

Comprehensive KPI framework tracking 35+ metrics across the revenue funnel from awareness to closed-won. Includes lead lifecycle glossary, reverse funnel calculator, and dashboard specifications.

**Key Proof Points:** LTV:CAC target >3:1, Pipeline Coverage target 3-4x, 1.2% end-to-end conversion example

---

### 4. BDR Salesforce Workflow Guide
**File:** `bdr-salesforce-workflow-guide.md`

Operational guide for BDRs using Salesforce Lightning Essentials. Covers daily workflows, lead management, campaign tracking, bulk operations, and reporting.

**Key Proof Points:** 200 records max bulk tasks, 6 daily time blocks, 5 lead status stages

---

### 5. CX/BPO Repositioning Initiative
**File:** `cxbpo-repositioning-initiative.md`

Strategic initiative to transform CX/BPO messaging from conservative to bold positioning targeting millennial/Gen-Z buyers. "Radical Relevance" framework with provocative hooks.

**Key Proof Points:** 95%+ CSAT, <10% agent attrition (vs 30%+ industry), 100% AI QA coverage

---

### 6. Healthcare RCM Denials Automation (PULSE/SAGA)
**File:** `healthcare-rcm-pulse-saga-automation.md`

Messaging framework for Healthcare RCM offering focused on PULSE/SAGA automation for claims denials. Value proposition matrix and expansion to $500 minimum claim size.

**Key Proof Points:** Up to 26 days faster appeals, ~47% approval improvement, $500 minimum claims

### 7. DebtNext Cross-Sell Strategy
**File:** `debtnext-cross-sell-strategy.md`

Strategic framework for converting DebtNext software customers into TSI services clients. Cross-sell narratives, pilot structure, and BDR messaging kits.

**Key Proof Points:** 90-day carve-out pilots, 1 pilot/month goal, modeled meaningful uplift from bundling

### 8. Revenue Funnel Self-Assessment
**File:** `revenue-funnel-self-assessment.md`

Diagnostic framework evaluating TSI's revenue operations maturity across 30+ KPIs. 1-5 scoring rubric with gap prioritization methodology.

**Key Proof Points:** 30+ KPIs assessed, 5 operational categories, candid process gap identification

## Usage Notes
These stubs are designed for portfolio presentation and interview preparation. They emphasize:
- **Proof points** - Specific numbers and percentages
- **Attribution clarity** - What Connor built vs. what TSI provided
- **Strategic impact** - Business outcomes and operational improvements

For full case study development, add:
- Client/partner quotes (if available)
- Before/after performance comparisons
- Visual assets (screenshots, dashboards, deck slides)
- Timeline details and stakeholder feedback
